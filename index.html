Lyric Video Maker

Turn your audio and lyrics into a stunning visual experience.

How to Use Lyric Video Maker

1

Prepare Audio

Upload a high-quality audio file (.mp3 or .wav). If you need to isolate or clean up the audio first, you can use vocalremover.org.

2

Get Lyrics

Obtain a synchronized lyric file (.lrc or .ttml). You can easily find synced lyrics for any song at Youly Plus Find Lyrics.

3

Customize & Design

Add a background image or album cover. Choose your font, adjust text colors, add a glowing effect, and set your credits name.

4

Export Video

Preview the video in real-time. When satisfied, click "Generate & Download Video" to export your masterpiece as a high-quality video file.
