How to Use Lyric Video Maker

1

Prepare Audio

Upload a high-quality audio file (.mp3 or .wav). If you need to isolate or clean up the audio first, you can use vocalremover.org.

2

Get Lyrics

Obtain a synchronized lyric file (.lrc or .ttml). You can easily find synced lyrics for any song at Youly Plus Find Lyrics.

3

Customize & Design

Add a background image or album cover. Choose your font, adjust text colors, add a glowing effect, and set your credits name.

4

Export Video

Preview the video in real-time. When satisfied, click "Generate & Download Video" to export your masterpiece as a high-quality video file.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the video frame rate (FPS) really low?

Since rendering and video compression occur in real-time inside your browser, the frame rate depends on how much CPU and GPU power is available. If your device is older, busy, or underpowered, it may skip frames. We recommend closing other browser tabs and heavy apps during export.

Why does it play the full song when I export?

To capture both the audio and video tracks in sync without uploading your files to a server, the browser must play and record the media stream in real-time from start to finish. Fast-forwarding is not supported by standard browser recording APIs.

Why does it depend on my hardware?

To keep this application completely free and protect your privacy, all rendering and encoding are computed locally on your device. Zero data is ever sent to a remote server, meaning the speed is limited only by your computer's hardware capability.

Does it matter what browser I use?

Yes, browser support varies! Modern Chromium-based browsers (Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Brave, Opera) offer the best performance and codec compatibility. Mozilla Firefox is also supported, but Chrome or Edge are highly recommended for the smoothest exports.

Does this app send my media files to any servers?

No! This is a static site hosted entirely on GitHub Pages. We do not own or run any backend servers. All processing is done 100% locally in your browser. If any connection or hosting logs are collected, they are handled by GitHub itself. Please refer to the GitHub Privacy Statement for more details.

Can I use this program offline?

Yes! Once the page is loaded, you can disconnect from the internet completely and the application will continue to work perfectly. This confirms that all rendering, audio processing, and video generation occur entirely offline on your local computer.

I don't have a lyric file for my song

You can search for pre-synchronized lyric files using the resources recommended in our guide. Alternatively, you can write the synchronization tags yourself in the Live Editor, or use the interactive AMLL TTML Tool to easily sync lyrics word-by-word.

Will you ever support automatic audio transcription?

No, we will not. In order to keep this application 100% serverless (running entirely offline on your own device), we do not send your audio files to cloud-based AI transcription servers. We recommend using the tools recommended in our guide to find or generate synchronized lyric files beforehand.

What format is the video exported as and why?

Videos are exported as standard WebM (or MP4 in some browsers). We utilize the browser's built-in MediaRecorder API to compile the canvas frames and audio stream directly on your computer. This provides high-quality compression and fast processing without needing any external server-side video rendering software.
